ABOUT US

Technical Club - FLUX is the official Technical club of S.A.T.I. Vidisha previously known as Innovation club - FLUX. Our club in a nutshell makes cool projects, organises workshops/events while doing a lot of other fun activities. We mainly focus on latest and future demanding technologies like Internet of Things, Machine Learning, Artificial Intelligence, and many more.

EVENT DESCRIPTION :-

Technical Club Flux is going to recruit members for the academic year 2022-23. And we invite all the students who are interested in working on technical, robotics and innovative projects.
There are three processes in which this recruitment will be conducted. The four processes are explained below in detail.

RECRUITMENT PROCESSES

1) Register

Do register yourself by filling the google form https://forms.gle/b4damb8ZyLTwm9Pr9 Last date of registration is 8 October 2022.
Fill the registration form carefully, since next round tasks will be assigned on that basis.

2) Get your task done before deadline

After filling the registration form, the tasks will be assigned via mail. Before deadline, submit the tasks. The last date of submission will also be assigned along with task.

3) Personal Interview and Task Submission

On the basis of task submission, shortlisted candidates will be called for Personal Interview. The date of personal interview will be shared via mail.

CONCLUSION

Based on the performance of candidates in process 1, process 2 and process 3, a list having the names of the selected candidates will be issued.
